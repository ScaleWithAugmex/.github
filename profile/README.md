## Solutions We Have Built

Many of these platforms started as real client problems.

- **Radar** — B2B prospect intelligence. Researches accounts, finds decision makers, drafts outreach.
- **CRM** — Customer relationship platform with AI deal scoring and workflow automation.
- **AI Chatbot** — Knowledge-base support with retrieval-augmented generation and human handoff.
- **C-Auditor** — Content, SEO, and AI-readiness auditing for search and AI discovery.
- **HRM** — Workforce platform for recruitment, payroll, onboarding, and performance reviews.
- **Black Box** — Enterprise AI gateway for LLM routing, caching, governance, and cost control.
- **Signal** — Market intelligence from public conversations and competitive data.
- **Prism** — Analytics assistant built on GA4 and Search Console data.
- **Career Platform** — AI-assisted hiring and applicant screening.
- **MayaOS** — Bangla-first ERP for FMCG, inventory, distribution, and accounting.
- **Jewen** — Call analysis with summaries, sentiment, coaching, and compliance.
- **FlowIVR** — Voice AI receptionist that answers calls and qualifies leads.
- **adda** — Team workspace with chat, projects, wiki, docs, and whiteboards.
